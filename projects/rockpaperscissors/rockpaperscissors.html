How to play Rock, Paper, Scissors

Start by selecting "Start", by doing this it will bring up to options to chose from "Standard" and "Hard". After selecting from the difficulties you will enter the game. Select Rock Paper or Scissors and the results will be shown, hit replay and keep playing, whoever gets a score of three will win the set, this in which will bring up the endscreen which will allow you to play again or exit.
